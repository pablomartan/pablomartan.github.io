Climber's Crag

Summary

Barranquet del Ferri is a very nice crag in Alcoi. Surrounded by trees, very near town, and with plenty of sectors and routes to choose from. Fun climbing waranteed!

Introduction

Barranquet del Ferri is a very nice crag in Alcoi. At a distance of only 4.1Km from the town's center, the cliff has some very nice sectors. The one we climbed at was «Paret esquerra», or «Left wall». The crag is a cliff that's hidden behind a mountain. The way that leads to it beggins surrounded by trees, later it takes you through a small valley, and later some rocks.

Avaliable crags

The cliff itself has some vegetation, a cave and five sectors with bolted routes. They are Sector del principi 1 i 2 , Sector Esbarzer , Sector Paret Dreta i Esquerra , Sector Cova , Sector Jimi , Sector Talladits i Sector Novato.

The routes are a little sandbagged, especially the older ones, but they are delightful, plus the sun hits in one wall in the morning and the other one in the afternoon, so you can climb there all day!

In this wall there were the easiest routes of the left and right wall sectors, including a III+ in the French grading system, which makes it perfect for people who are just starting.

The sun hits this wall during the morning, so depending on the time of the year you are visiting, it would be preferable to climb this wall on the evening, so that you don't burn your hands!

Closing

If you want to visit this crag, make sure to check out this website where you can find most information regarding sectors, how to find it, and what to expect. Happy climbing!

Don't forget to pick up after yourself! Even fruit leftovers are considered trash. Remember to bring a bag with yourself so that you can leave the crag clean for the other climber's to enjoy.
